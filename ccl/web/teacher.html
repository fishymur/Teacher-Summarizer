Curriculum contract

The contract is the set of rules your tutor follows for this course: which method students should use, which method to hold back until a later unit, anything never allowed, and how much help the tutor may give in each mode. Compile a draft from your materials, adjust it, approve it, and publish. The tutor obeys only what you publish.
